Birth Month Flowers Part 2

When it comes to birth month flowers most months will have a primary and secondary flower due to different cultures or availability. Birth flowers are silimar to Birthstones in the sense that they are designated to a certain month, typically based on the season they are in bloom.

April

The Primary flower for the month of April is a Daisy, with the secondary flower being a Sweet Pea. Depending on the color the Daisy can represent multiple things, the most popular ones being white, which represent innocence and purity. Yellow dasies represent friendship, pink represents affection and red represents love. Sweet Peas can be used to say "Thank you for a lovely time".

May

The Primary flower for the month of May is a Lily of the Valley, with the secondary flower being a Hawthorn. Lily of the Valley is white flower, meaning it represents purity and sweetness. They also represent "the return of happiness". Hawthorns, despite thorns being in the name represent hope.

June

TThe Primary flower for the month of June is a Rose, with the secondary flower being a Honeysuckle. Everyone knows Roses are known for their symbolism of love, not just romantic love either. Honeysuckles represent happiness and positive energy given it's bright yellow color.
