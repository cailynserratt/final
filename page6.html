Birth Month Flowers Part 4

When it comes to birth month flowers most months will have a primary and secondary flower due to different cultures or availability. Birth flowers are silimar to Birthstones in the sense that they are designated to a certain month, typically based on the season they are in bloom.

October

The Primary flower for the month of October is a Marigold, with the secondary flower being a Cosmos. Following the October month with the Halloween spirt, Marigolds are known to represent death and grief, used commenly in Day of the Dead festivities. Cosmos symbolize peace, innocence, and luck.

November

The Primary flower for the month of November is a Chrysanthemum, with there being no secondary flower. Although November is the only month not to have a secondray flower, Chrysanthemums represent loyalty, friendship and joy.

December

TThe Primary flower for the month of December is a Narcissus, with the secondary flower being a Holly. Finally, December has the destive flowers commenly used in holiday decorations. Holly represents domestic happiness, while the Narcissus symbolizes innocence and purity.
