The Color Of the Flowers Meaning
Red Flowers Associated with love, passion, courage
Yellow Flowers Associated with joy, friendship, happiness
Pink Flowers Associated with femininity, love, gentleness
Orange Flowers Associated with excitement, jealousy, energetic
White Flowers Associated with innocence, purity, sympathy
Blue Flowers Associated with relaxation, peace, calm
Purple(dark) Flowers Associated with royalty, success, admiration
Purple(light) Flowers Associated with grace, youth, elegance
Green Flowers Associated with rebirth, renewal
