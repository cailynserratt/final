Birth Month Flowers Part 3

When it comes to birth month flowers most months will have a primary and secondary flower due to different cultures or availability. Birth flowers are silimar to Birthstones in the sense that they are designated to a certain month, typically based on the season they are in bloom.

July

The Primary flower for the month of July is a Larkspur, with the secondary flower being a Water Lily. Larkspurs, despite being poisonous, are th symbol of positivity and dedication. The secondary flower, Water lilies, represent purity and innocence.

August

The Primary flower for the month of August is a Gladiolus, with the secondary flower being a Poppy. Gladiolus, a symbol of love and strength with its bright colors. Contrary to the positivity of Gladiolus, the Poppy is known for being a symbol of grief and mourning due to the loss of another.

September

The Primary flower for the month of September is a Aster, with the secondary flower being a Morning Glory. Like August, Asters also are a symbol to remember those we have lost, Asters also symbolize love and daintiness. Morning Glory, contrary to the glory, symbolize love that is unrequited and undying just like vines.
